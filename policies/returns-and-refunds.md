# Returns & Refunds

## Refund window

We accept refund requests within **30 days of the order date**. Requests made
after 30 days cannot be refunded through support; day 30 itself still
qualifies.

## What you get back

Approved refunds return the **full order amount to the original payment
method**. Refunds are processed as soon as a member of our team approves the
request; your bank may take 3–5 business days to show the money.

## High-value orders

Refunds over **$100** receive an additional review by our team before they are
processed. This can add up to one business day.

## Condition of items

Items should be unused and in their original packaging. If an item arrived
damaged or defective, mention it in your request — damaged-on-arrival items
are always refundable within the 30-day window.
