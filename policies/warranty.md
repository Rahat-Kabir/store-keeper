# Warranty

## Coverage

All products carry a **12-month warranty** against manufacturing defects,
counted from the delivery date. Kitchen appliances (for example espresso
machines) are covered for the same 12 months.

## What is covered

- Defects in materials or workmanship.
- Products that stop working under normal home use.

## What is not covered

- Normal wear and tear (scratches, fading).
- Damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized repair.
- Consumable parts such as filters.

## How to claim

Contact support with your order number, a short description of the problem,
and a photo if possible. Valid claims receive a replacement, or a refund if
the product is no longer available.
