# Cancellations

## When an order can be cancelled

An order can be cancelled **only while it is unfulfilled** — that is, before
it has shipped. Once an order has shipped (fully or partially), it can no
longer be cancelled; use a return and refund instead once it arrives.

## What happens on cancellation

Cancelled orders are refunded in full to the original payment method, and any
reserved stock is released. You will not be charged anything for a cancelled
order.

## How to cancel

Reply to your order confirmation or contact support with your order number
(for example `#1036`). Cancellation requests are reviewed by our team before
they are carried out.
