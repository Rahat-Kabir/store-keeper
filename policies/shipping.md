# Shipping

## Processing time

Orders are processed within **1–2 business days**. You will receive a
confirmation email with tracking once your order ships.

## Delivery estimates

- United States: 3–7 business days after processing.
- We currently ship within the United States only.

## Tracking

Every shipment includes a tracking number, sent by email when the order
leaves our warehouse. If your tracking has not updated for more than 5
business days, contact support with your order number.

## Lost or delayed packages

If a package is marked delivered but has not arrived, or is more than 10
business days late, contact support — we will investigate with the carrier
and arrange a replacement or refund where appropriate.
